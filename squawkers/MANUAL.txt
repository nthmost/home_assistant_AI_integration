## 1. GETTING TO KNOW YOUR SQUAWKERS McCAW PARROT

SQUAWKERS McCAW is an interactive parrot toy that responds to touch, voice commands, and remote control inputs. He comes with multiple sensors, can learn custom phrases, and interacts in various play modes.

### FOR PARROT:
- 4 x 1.5V "AA" or LR6 size alkaline batteries (not included)

### FOR REMOTE CONTROL:
- 2 x 1.5V "AAA" or R03 size alkaline batteries (not included)

## 3. BATTERY INSTALLATION

### Remote Control Battery Installation:
The first thing you need to do when you take your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot out of the box is to insert batteries in the Remote Control.

1. Use a Phillips/cross head screwdriver (not included) to loosen the screw in the battery compartment cover (screw stays attached to cover)
2. Remove cover
3. Insert 2 x 1.5V "AAA" or R03 size batteries (not included). Alkaline batteries recommended
4. Replace cover and tighten screw

### Parrot Battery Installation:
1. Using a Phillips head screwdriver (not included), loosen screw in battery compartment (screw stays attached to cover)
2. Insert 4 x 1.5V "AA" or LR6 size alkaline batteries (not included)
3. Replace cover and tighten screw

### BATTERY SAFETY CAUTIONS:
1. As with all small batteries, the batteries used with this toy should be kept away from children who still put things in their mouths. If swallowed, promptly see a doctor and have the doctor phone (202) 625-3333 collect. In other countries, have the doctor call your local poison control center.
2. Make sure the batteries are inserted correctly and always follow the toy and battery manufacturer's instructions.
3. Do not mix old batteries and new batteries or alkaline, standard (carbon-zinc) or rechargeable (nickel-cadmium) batteries.
4. Always follow the instructions carefully. Use only batteries specified and be sure to insert items correctly by matching the + and - polarity markings.
5. Remove exhausted or dead batteries from the product.
6. Remove batteries if product is not to be played with for a long time.
7. Do not short-circuit the supply terminals.
8. Should this product cause, or be affected by, local electrical interference, move it away from other electrical equipment. Reset (switching off and back on again or removing and reinserting batteries) if necessary.
9. RECHARGEABLE BATTERIES: Do not mix these with any other types of batteries. Always remove from the product before recharging. Recharge batteries under adult supervision. DO NOT RECHARGE OTHER TYPES OF BATTERIES.

**Please retain this information for future reference. Batteries should be replaced by an adult.**

## 4. ASSEMBLY AND SETUP

### Assembling the Perch:
Your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot comes with his own perch, packaged in three pieces. The perch, once assembled, cannot be taken apart.

1. Insert each piece of the perch at a 90-degree angle (or, a quarter-turn) from the other piece; then turn the inserted piece clockwise until it clicks into place
2. Line up the feet of SQUAWKERS McCAW with the pegs, and attach him to the perch

**Note:** SQUAWKERS McCAW can sit on the perch facing either direction. He will also function standing on a flat surface, such as a table or desk.

### Locking/Unlocking from Perch:
- The feet lock to the perch automatically when attached
- When you want to take the parrot off the perch, push up on the button to release

## 5. TURNING YOUR SQUAWKERS McCAW PARROT ON

1. Locate the DEMO/OFF/ON Switch (located on the back)
2. Set to the "ON" position
3. When you turn SQUAWKERS McCAW on, he will "wake up" making sounds and moving his wings!

**Important:** When you turn the switch back to "ON," you erase all words and responses previously recorded.

## 6. PLAY MODES AND INTERACTION

### Hangout Mode (Default Mode):
When you "wake up" your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot, he starts in Hangout Mode. He's just relaxing and hanging out! In Hangout Mode, SQUAWKERS McCAW will:
- Make sounds as he waits
- Respond when you touch any of his sensors
- Wait for more interaction

Your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot loves to play and interact with you! Just like a real bird!

## 7. SENSORS AND RESPONSES

### Light Sensor (Forehead):
Your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot has a Light Sensor on his forehead. Wave your hand over his head and SQUAWKERS McCAW will blink.

### Back Sensor:
When you pet the back of SQUAWKERS McCAW, he will coo and move in response to your touch. You can also relax SQUAWKERS McCAW by petting his head firmly.

### Head Sensor:
Pet his head firmly to relax him or get him out of a Play Mode.

### Beak Sensor:
Pet the beak of your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot, and watch him either coo or give you a parrot kiss.

### Feeding SQUAWKERS McCAW:
SQUAWKERS McCAW comes with a toy cracker. When you place the toy cracker on the tongue of SQUAWKERS McCAW, he will:
- Chew on the toy cracker
- Make yummy sounds
- If you feed him too much at once, he'll get the hiccups
- Pat his back, and he'll burp and feel better
- Remember: be careful of your fingers around a bird's mouth!

## 8. VOICE COMMANDS AND RECOGNITION

### Tips for Best Voice Recognition:
- Before you speak, wait until your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot has finished speaking
- Reduce background noise
- Speak clearly and distinctly. Don't shout
- If SQUAWKERS McCAW doesn't respond in the way you expect, try speaking again
- When SQUAWKERS McCAW is in a Play Mode (Dance Mode or Repeat Mode), he cannot respond to your voice commands. Pet his head to get him out of a Play Mode if you want to speak to him
- For best results, speak to SQUAWKERS McCAW from about 1 to 2 feet away

If you say something to SQUAWKERS McCAW and he either did not hear or understand you, he will blink at you. Speak again and this time, be clearer or change the tone of your voice.

### Peek-a-Boo Game:
1. Cup your hand over the head or eyes of SQUAWKERS McCAW and say "Peek-a-boo!"
2. He'll reply, "Where'd you go?" or "I can't see you"
3. If you uncover his eyes and say "Peek-a-boo!" SQUAWKERS McCAW will say, "I see you!" or "Peek-a-boo!"

### Dance Mode:
1. Make sure SQUAWKERS McCAW is ready by listening for a cue
2. Wait for SQUAWKERS McCAW to say "OK!"
3. Ask, "Wanna dance?" and your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot will let you know he's ready to dance by saying, "Turn up the music!"
4. He will dance either by: (1) pressing the appropriate button on the Remote Control, or (2) following the beat
5. If SQUAWKERS McCAW does not detect a beat for more than eight (8) seconds, he will start dancing to his own beat

### Repeat Mode:
In this mode, your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot will repeat what you say.
1. Say "Polly!" and SQUAWKERS McCAW will say "Yeah?" or "Hello!"
2. SQUAWKERS McCAW will repeat what you say once, in a silly parrot-like voice
3. He will then repeat what you say to you
4. SQUAWKERS McCAW will keep repeating what you say until you stop talking to him
5. After 1 minute of inactivity in Repeat Mode, he will return to Hangout Mode

**Note:** To get out of Repeat Mode or Dance Mode before eight (8) seconds of inactivity, pet his head or press RESET on the Remote Control.

## 9. RECORDING CUSTOM COMMANDS AND RESPONSES

### What You Can Record:
You can program your SQUAWKERS McCAW to:
1. Say any words and phrases you want
2. Say certain words/phrases when you press a lettered button on the Remote Control (A-F)
3. Say certain words/phrases when he hears you say certain words/phrases

You can create custom commands and responses using the A-F buttons on the Remote Control.

**Important:** Commands recorded to a preset button are linked to responses recorded for the same button. For example, if you record the command "Hi, Squawkers!" to button A and record the response "Hey, good lookin'!" to button A, Squawkers will respond with "Hey, good lookin'!" every time he hears the command "Hi, Squawkers!"

**Note:** Until you add your own commands, responses, and random vocabulary, when you press the lettered buttons on the Remote Control, your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot will respond using his preset vocabulary. When you pet his head, and he will blink. You will hear a squawk to let you know when SQUAWKERS McCAW is ready. If you press the incorrect button, press and release the button a second time to record over your mistake.

### Recording Commands:
1. Set the Content Selector Switch to "Custom Speech"
2. Set the Remote Control switch to "Command"
3. Press and hold one of the six (6) preset buttons on the lettered keypad
4. SQUAWKERS McCAW will squawk to let you know that he's ready to record
5. Release the button, and speak a word or phrase (up to 25 seconds) immediately into the microphone, located in the chest
6. Wait for SQUAWKERS McCAW to squawk again, then say the word or phrase a second time
7. SQUAWKERS McCAW will repeat the word or phrase back to you
8. If you would like to change the command, you can record over it at any time

**Note:** SQUAWKERS McCAW will respond only to commands spoken by the same person who programmed the command. If you are teaching a command to repeat a preset button, press and release the button. If you release too quickly, SQUAWKERS McCAW will repeat the command.

### Recording Responses:
1. Set the Remote Control switch to "Response"
2. Press and hold one of the six (6) preset buttons on the lettered keypad
3. SQUAWKERS McCAW will squawk to let you know that he's ready to record
4. Release the button, and speak a word or phrase (up to 3 seconds long) immediately into the microphone, located in the chest
5. SQUAWKERS McCAW will repeat the word or phrase back to you
6. If you would like to change the response, you can record over it at any time

### Recording Random Vocabulary:
1. Press and hold the Custom Record button on the Remote Control. SQUAWKERS McCAW will squawk to let you know that he's ready to record
2. Release the button and speak a word or phrase (up to 3 seconds) immediately into the microphone in the chest
3. SQUAWKERS McCAW will repeat the word or phrase back to you

These words and phrases (either "Parrot Talk Speech" or "Custom Speech" as indicated by the Content Selector Switch) become part of the parrot's vocabulary. SQUAWKERS McCAW will say these words or phrases at random.

To scroll through and hear what you've recorded:
- Press and release the Custom Record button to scroll through recorded vocabulary
- When you hear the word or phrase you want to record over, press and hold the Custom Record button and say the new word or phrase

### A Word About Words:
Please be careful when teaching your SQUAWKERS McCAW parrot words and phrases. SQUAWKERS McCAW learns like a child learns language. SQUAWKERS McCAW depends on you to teach him nice things to say. Remember, too, that SQUAWKERS McCAW can copy you, so be mindful!

If the product is acting erratically, try turning it off and on again. If problems persist, change the batteries in the parrot and/or remote.
